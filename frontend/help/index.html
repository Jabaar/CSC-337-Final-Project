Help

How to create an account?

1. Navigate to the login page. Switch from the login page to the create account page (sign up button underlined).

2. Enter your username and password (fields marked below).

How to search for an article?

1. Login to your account.

2. Enter what you are searching for in the top left corner of your browser (search box marked below).

3. Hit enter or click the search button.

How to update account settings?

1. Login to your account.

2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner of your browser (icon marked below).

3. Make any chnages to your name and click save.

How to change your profile picture?

1. Login to your account.

2. Click the profile picture in the top right corner of your browser (icon marked below).

3. Select the file button, then choose and file and upload (buttons marked).

How to logout?

1. Login to your account.

2. Click the profile picture in the top right corner of your browser (icon marked below).

3. Click the button in the top right conner (button marked below / button subject to change).

How to make a comment?

1. Login to your account.

2. Click the show comments button (marked below).

3. Enter your comment in the text box and hit post (marked below).

How to reply to a comment?

1. Login to your account.

2. Click the show comments button (marked below).

3. Click the reply button on the comment you want to reply to.

4. Add a reply in the text box and hit post (marked below).

How to delete a comment?

1. Login to your account.

2. Click the show comments button (marked below).

3. Click the delete button on the comment you want to delete (marked below).
